Swing Outs: Foot work variations

In this class we focused on swingouts with variations of the footwork. In all cases we start with a back kick on 7, a front kick on 8, and then three options for beats 1 and 2:

  • Rock step
  • Double ball change
  • Back kick on 1, ball change on 2

Overrotated Swingouts

Following on from Straight Swingouts and Rotated Swingouts, in this session we focused on Overrotated Swingouts. We looked at a couple different foot work variations to use during the continuous rotation.

  • Suzie Qs
  • Tacky Annies'
  • Kick ball changes
